Lost in Shanghai



Spots

There many interesting spots in Shanghai.If you like to see beautiful night scenery, go to The Bund. The bund is famous for its night view. Consructions are shining in the night.

Xintiandi is also a very popular place which is welcomed. In that area, you can not only enjoy the old Shanghai feeling but also crashed with the modern Shanghai. There are delicious food, old construction style called Shikumen, all the houses are built with stones.

Tianzifang is the a popular spot in Shanghai.There are many characteristic stores along the street. You can buy some special gifts for your friends and families there.


Shopping

Shopping malls fills in everywhere in Shanghai. If you wanna big shopping mall, you can go to Jiuguang or K11. If you want to some special stores you can go to Xintiandi. There are many designer brands.


Food

Food food food! Food is always a big part of the trip. Chinese food is the most important part you can not miss. Shengjiaobao, is my favourite food. It is a kind of dumping with a fried bottom. Dazhaxie is another favourite thing. It is a kind of crab which only provided in Sep and Oct. Highly recommend!
